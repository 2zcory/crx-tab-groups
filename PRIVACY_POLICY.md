# Privacy Policy for Groupify: Auto Tab Organizer

Last updated: 2026-06-24

Groupify ("we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains that Groupify does not collect, transmit, store, or share any personal data, user credentials, or web browsing history.

## 1. Information Collection & Use
Groupify is designed to run entirely client-side.
- **No Remote Servers**: We do not maintain any external servers, databases, or analytics engines.
- **No Data Collection**: We do not collect, monitor, or track your browsing activity, visited URLs, personal identity, search queries, or any other web-based information.
- **Minimum Privilege**: We only request necessary permissions to interact with browser tabs and groups locally on your machine.

## 2. Data Storage
All configurations, automation rules, and tab snapshots you create are saved locally on your device via Chrome's storage API.
- **Local Storage**: Settings are kept within the extension's local storage sandbox.
- **Chrome Sync Storage**: If you are signed in to your Google Account with sync enabled, Chrome will synchronize these settings securely to your other Google-authenticated devices. We do not have access to or control over this synchronized data.

## 3. Data Sharing and Third-Party Access
We do not sell, trade, rent, or share any user data with third parties. No data is used for advertising, marketing, profiling, creditworthiness, or any other commercial purposes.

## 4. Compliance with Google Web Store Policies
Groupify complies with the Chrome Web Store Developer Agreement and User Data Policy, ensuring that all data usage matches the declared single purpose of auto-organizing tab groups and managing snapshots.
